postdoctoral fellowships

There are several fellowships you can apply for and the MSCA Postdoctoral Fellowship (for researchers max. 8 years post-PhD, who currently reside outside the Netherlands). deadlines are early September 2025.

  • Radboud Excellence Postdoctoral Fellowship:. 2-year position, University-wide fellowship with 2-stage process (deadline 10 October 2024)
  • Veni fellowship:. Dutch fellowship €320.000,- for 3-year appointment, available for researchers max. 3 years post-PhD (dealine for pre-proposals 3 September 2024)
  • Marie Skłodowska-Curie fellowship: Prestigious European fellowship for 2-year position (deadline 10 September 2024)

About Radboud University Nijmegen

The Department of Astrophysics is part of Institute for Mathematics, Astrophysics and Particle Physics and research, and the Dutch network of astronomy institutes NOVA. Research topics include black holes and neutron stars, astroparticle physics and gravity waves, as well as how objects in a galactic environment form and evolve.

The Netherlands boasts one of the most prestigious university systems in Europe and, more importantly, an inclusive and international environment. Learn more about the research and facilities on the RU website.
