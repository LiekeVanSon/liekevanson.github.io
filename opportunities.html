About Radboud University Nijmegen

The Department of Astrophysics is part of Institute for Mathematics, Astrophysics and Particle Physics and research, and the Dutch network of astronomy institutes NOVA. Research topics include black holes and neutron stars, astroparticle physics and gravity waves, as well as how objects in a galactic environment form and evolve.

The Netherlands boasts one of the most prestigious university systems in Europe and, more importantly, an inclusive and international environment. Learn more about the research and facilities on the RU website.
