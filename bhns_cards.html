Hunting for black holes & neutron stars:
the card deck

Two decks: the tell-tale phenomena that reveal compact objects, and the observatories we hunt them with. Your task: figure out which instruments can catch your phenomena — and how.

In class: each group gets two object cards (e.g. “A1 & D3”). You have ~20 minutes: What is the signal? Which observatories could detect it, and what would they measure? Click an observatory card to see its specs. Then teach it to the rest of us. Finished early? Grab an extra card.

Built for the School on Gravity 2026 · Lieke van Son. Mission-time bars run 1990–2042 (hatched = planned/under construction; the tick marks today). Spectral bars are log-frequency (EM), log-GW-frequency, or log-neutrino-energy. Observatory images live in bhns_card_images/ (see each card's Wikipedia link for sources/credits). References link to NASA ADS.
